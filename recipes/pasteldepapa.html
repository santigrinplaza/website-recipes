Pastel De Papa Recipe

Description

Pastel de Papa is a comforting and hearty dish from Latin American cuisine, particularly popular in Chile and Argentina. It’s essentially a savory casserole made with layers of creamy mashed potatoes, ground meat, and onions. The filling typically combines beef or a mixture of beef and pork, seasoned with garlic, paprika, and cumin to infuse it with rich flavors. Some variations also include olives, hard-boiled eggs, and raisins, adding a delightful contrast in taste and texture. The mashed potatoes are prepared with butter, milk, and sometimes cheese, creating a smooth, velvety base that balances the savory filling.

To assemble the pastel de papa, a layer of mashed potatoes is spread in a baking dish, followed by the seasoned meat mixture. Another layer of mashed potatoes tops the filling, and the casserole is baked until golden brown and crispy on top. The result is a comforting, satisfying dish that can be served as a main course or a side. Whether enjoyed on a special occasion or as a simple family meal, pastel de papa offers a perfect combination of flavors and textures, making it a beloved dish in many homes across Latin America.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs (900g) potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup black olives (optional)
  • 2 boiled eggs, sliced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes: Peel and chop the potatoes, then cook them in boiling water until tender (about 15-20 minutes). Drain and mash them with milk, butter, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook the meat: Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onions and minced garlic until soft. Add the ground beef (or mixed meat) and cook until browned. Season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add extra ingredients to the meat: Optionally, stir in olives, boiled egg slices, and raisins into the meat mixture.
  4. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  5. Layer the casserole: Spread a layer of mashed potatoes at the bottom of a greased baking dish. Add the meat mixture on top, then cover with the remaining mashed potatoes. Optionally, sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  6. Bake: Place the assembled pastel de papa in the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden and crispy.
  7. Serve: Let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
